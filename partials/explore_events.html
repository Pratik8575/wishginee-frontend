What is an Event?

An event can be described as a public assembly for the purpose of celebration, education, marketing or reunion.


How to create Event?

Only NGO and public user can create Events

For that just go through the page of Events.

Then click on the SETUP Event button to create Event.

After that fill all the details in form. All fields are required.


How to Donate?

To Donate to specific event just go through the Donation button at bottom of that event. And then go through the instructions.


Why Events are necessary?

  • The advantages of a event alliance include: Stronger voice and wider reach: making a event known more widely, and enhancing its credibility both with the target audience and potential supporters.
  • With a broad alliance, a event can draw support from diverse constituencies and networks.
