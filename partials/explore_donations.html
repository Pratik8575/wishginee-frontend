How to Donate?

The Donation procedure is safe and sound. The reason for its safety is because Wishginee uses RazorPay - An Indian Startup for Online Transaction as it's online transaction partner. To proceed any donation, following steps to be kept in mind-

  1. Search and explore any campaign/event which came from approval of admin.
  2. Click on Donate where you will be redirected to RazorPay Portal for pay.
  3. Enter your details and that's it! You donated for a campaign/event.
